- **Legal Compliance**: Age verification and content restrictions vary by region

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Payment**: Confirm acceptance before Phase 2, identify alternatives
- **Legal**: Complete compliance review in Phase 2
- **Suppliers**: Establish relationships in Phase 1, identify backups
- **SEO**: Focus on wellness keywords, implement schema markup
